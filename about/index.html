School of Engineering

Building a better world through a broad-based, multidisciplinary approach at the most established engineering school.

Ngee Ann Polytechnic has been an established name in engineering education since 1967. Always keeping in times with the needs of the industry, the current unified School of Engineering (SoE) offers diverse fields in engineering and multiple learning pathways to cater to the needs of the students.

With its strong ties to the industry, SoE offers the latest technologies and great exposure to real-world learning, thus equipping our graduates with sought-after engineering skills and knowledge.

Our top-notch facilities, coupled with competent academic staff, promises an enriching education.
